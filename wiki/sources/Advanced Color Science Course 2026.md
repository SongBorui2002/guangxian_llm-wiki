## Overview

Educational course material covering advanced color science concepts including color metamerism failure, observer differences, light source effects, and the physiological and instrumental bases of color vision.

## Key Topics Covered

- Color Metamerism Failure (同色异谱失效)
- Physiological Color Matching Function (生理CMF)
- CIE Standard Observers (1931 vs 1964)
- Equipment Color Response Differences
- Viewing Angle Effects

## Relevance to Wiki

This source bridges the gap between theoretical color science and practical professional color grading workflows documented in the DaVinci Resolve domain. Understanding observer differences and metamerism is essential for:

- ACES color space conversions and proper IDT/ODT selection
- Cross-platform color matching in cinema workflows
- Managing color accuracy across different lighting conditions and viewing environments
- Troubleshooting color mismatches in DCP/IMF delivery
